    Resolution 2231(2015) was adopted on 20 July 2015, and, as required by the resolution, there are normally two UNSC "Non-Proliferation" meetings each year.

    Initially Iranian representatives did not attend. From 12 December 2018 they were invited to attend in accordance with UNSC Rule 37 of the Council's Provisional Rules of Procedure. At that meeting and meetings that followed the Iranian representatives delivered a statement.
